## Complete Feature List

### Core Storytelling Features

#### 1. Voice-First Interaction
- **Real-time conversation agent** for hands-free story design
- Natural language understanding - just describe what you want
- No menus, no typing required
- Designed for use in bed, in the car, or with hands full
- Remembers preferences across sessions

#### 2. Professional AI Narration
- **ElevenLabs premium voices** with emotional range
- 30+ narrator options from soothing to dramatic
- Character voice differentiation within stories
- Adjustable speed and tone
- Natural pauses and emphasis

#### 3. Multi-Agent Story Generation
- **8 specialized AI agents** working in concert:
  - Story Planner - structures compelling narratives
  - Lore Agent - maintains world consistency
  - Scene Writer - crafts vivid prose
  - Narration Director - optimizes for audio
  - Voice Agent - manages TTS integration
  - CYOA Manager - handles branching logic
  - Safety Agent - enforces content limits
  - Devil's Advocate - ensures creative quality

#### 4. Choose Your Own Adventure (CYOA)
- Interactive branching at key story moments
- Voice-activated choices ("Option one" or natural responses)
- Touch-friendly large buttons for manual selection
- Branch tracking and replay of alternate paths
- Dozens of possible endings per story

#### 5. Read-Along Karaoke Mode
- Synchronized text highlighting with narration
- Adjustable text size for different ages
- Word-by-word or phrase-by-phrase tracking
- Great for literacy development
- Follow complex plots visually

#### 6. Picture Book Mode
- AI-generated illustrations for each scene
- Multiple art styles (watercolor, cartoon, realistic, fantasy)
- Export stories as digital picture books
- Share illustrated stories as keepsakes
- Print-ready PDF export

#### 7. Story Library & Archive
- Every story saved automatically
- Re-listen to favorites anytime
- Continue CYOA stories from any branch point
- Share stories with family members
- Export audio files for offline listening
